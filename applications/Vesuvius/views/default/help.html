How to create a project

  1. You need an account to create a project on Vesuvius. You can log in or create an account from the ‘login’ button in the nav bar.
  2. Select the ‘create’ button in the nav bar.
  3. Fill out the required fields in the form. You can navigate back and forth to amend the fields.
  4. Choose your images and select the upload button to add the images to the website.
  5. After adding descriptions, press submit and you will be redirected to your dashboard where you can view your project.

How to transcribe a document image

  1. You need to be logged in to your account in order to transcribe. You can log in or create an account from the ‘login’ button in the nav bar.
  2. Search for a project by either selecting ‘browse’, or using the search field in the nav bar.
  3. On finding a project selecting ‘view’ will show all the document images in the project.
  4. Select ‘transcribe’ on any of the available document images.
  5. Fill out the requirements and press ‘submit’ to save and submit your transcription.
  6. You can view your submitted transcription on ‘My dashboard’ page, from the nav bar.

How to review submitted transcriptions

  1. You need to be logged in to your account to review submitted transcriptions.
  2. Your project needs to have transcriptions submitted by other users before you can review.
  3. Navigate to 'My Projects’ from the nav bar.
  4. Underneath the projects that have submitted transcriptions will have a button saying ‘review’ which you can select.
  5. On this page you can switch between the transcriptions and compare them, navigating using the arrows.
  6. You can choose to either accept one transcription, or reject all the transcriptions. You can change your mind after you have made your choice, by choosing accept on another transcription.
