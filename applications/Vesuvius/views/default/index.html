Welcome to Vesuvius, the historic crowd-sourced document transcription site!

There are several things you can do whilst on Vesuvius. If you are interested in historic documents you can view documents uploaded to the site and can contribute to historic discovery and digitisation by transcribing the documents you are viewing. If you have documents you want digitised, you can create a project and upload your images to allow other uses to transcribe it!
